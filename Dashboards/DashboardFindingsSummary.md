# Dashboard Findings Summary
## Dashboard 1: Executive Market Overview
- The Big Picture: Identified that the "Ultra-Luxury" segment drives 49% of market value despite representing only 11% of volume.
- Top 5 brands that dominate the inventory are Rolex, Omega, Seiko, Breitling and Cartier.

## Dashboard 2: Data Features Completeness
- Identified that almost 60% of watches "type of movement" data and 50% of "case material" data is missing
- Rolex which has the highest inventory in Volume shows critically missing "type of movement" data for each entry

## Dashboard 3: Inventory Health
- Rolex represents 21% of total platform volume, yet displays a remarkably narrow inventory breadth with only 0.4% distinct models.
- High price variance observed for Patek Phillipe and Richard Mille brands.
